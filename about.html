About Me

Passionate about technology, creativity and continuous learning.

Hello! I'm Faruk Shehu Zangina

I am currently studying Software Engineering at Northwest University, Kano. My passion for technology continues to grow as I explore web development, software engineering, graphic design and artificial intelligence.

My interest in software development began with curiosity about how websites and applications work. Since then, I have dedicated myself to learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript while building practical projects that improve my problem-solving skills.

Besides programming, I also enjoy graphic design where I create flyers, posters, logos and social media designs. Combining creativity with technology enables me to produce digital experiences that are both functional and visually appealing.

My long-term goal is to become a professional Full-Stack Software Engineer who develops innovative software solutions capable of solving real-world problems and making a positive impact through technology.
